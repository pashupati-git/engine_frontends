🧩 Key Widgets Built
exit_widgets.dart contains everything reusable: ExitAppHeader, ExitStepBar (animated dots), ExitSectionHeader, ExitCard, ExitReadOnlyField, ExitAutoFilledBadge, ExitCheckItem (animated checkboxes), ExitStarRatingRow (tappable stars), ExitFeedbackBlock, ExitSignatureArea, ExitNavFooter, ExitToast, and ExitSuccessView.

⚡ What the orchestrator handles
exit_form_screen.dart owns all state and coordinates everything — step progression, PageView with NeverScrollableScrollPhysics (swipe disabled, nav-only), validation (at least one reason required, signature required before submit), the animated toast, the loading spinner on submit, and the _buildPayload() method that already structures everything for your API.
